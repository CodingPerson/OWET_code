/LOCATION
A physical or geographical area or place , encompassing natural and man-made sites , regions , and features .

/ORGANIZATION
A group or entity formed for a specific purpose , such as business , governance , religion , or service , typically with a structured membership .

/PERSON
An individual human being , identified by name or other distinguishing characteristics .

/SUBSTANCE
A material or matter with a distinct physical presence , which can be natural or synthetic , including chemicals , drugs , and foods .

/GPE
A geopolitical entity , such as a country , state , province , or city , defined by political boundaries and governance .

/PRODUCT
A manufactured or crafted item intended for use , sale , or consumption , including vehicles and weapons .

/FACILITIES
Physical structures or complexes built for specific functions , such as buildings , airports , or bridges .

/DISEASE
A disorder or condition affecting living organisms , typically characterized by specific symptoms or signs .

/EVENT
A significant occurrence or happening , often with a specific time and place , such as wars or natural disasters .

/GAME
A structured form of play or competition , governed by rules , and often involving skill , strategy , or chance .

/LANGUAGE
A system of communication , spoken or written , used by a particular community or country .

/PLANT
A living organism of the kingdom Plantae , such as trees , flowers , or grasses .

/CONTACT_INFO
Information used to establish communication with a person or organization , such as phone numbers or email addresses .

/WORK_OF_ART
A creative or artistic work , such as books , plays , or songs , recognized for its cultural or aesthetic value .

/LAW
A rule or set of rules established by authority , governing conduct within a community or country .

/ANIMAL
A living organism of the kingdom Animalia , distinct from plants , fungi , and microorganisms .

/LOCATION/REGION
A broad geographical area , often defined by natural or cultural characteristics , but not necessarily by political boundaries (e .g . , the Midwest , the Sahara) .

/LOCATION/CONTINENT
One of the Earth's principal large landmasses , such as Africa , Asia , or Europe .

/LOCATION/LAKE_SEA_OCEAN
Large bodies of water , including lakes , seas , and oceans , distinguished by their size and ecological significance .

/LOCATION/RIVER
A natural flowing watercourse , usually freshwater , moving towards an ocean , sea , lake , or another river .

/ORGANIZATION/CORPORATION
A legally recognized business entity formed to conduct commercial activities , such as companies or enterprises .

/ORGANIZATION/GOVERNMENT
An entity responsible for governing a community , region , or country , including its administrative and executive branches .

/ORGANIZATION/POLITICAL
A group formed to influence or participate in political processes , such as political parties or advocacy groups .

/ORGANIZATION/HOTEL
An establishment providing lodging , meals , and other guest services for travelers and tourists .

/ORGANIZATION/RELIGIOUS
An entity organized around religious beliefs or practices , such as churches , temples , or religious orders .

/ORGANIZATION/HOSPITAL
An institution providing medical and surgical treatment and nursing care for sick or injured people .

/ORGANIZATION/EDUCATIONAL
An institution dedicated to education and learning , such as schools , colleges , or universities .

/SUBSTANCE/DRUG
A chemical or biological substance used for medical treatment , prevention , or diagnosis of diseases .

/SUBSTANCE/CHEMICAL
A pure substance or compound with a defined chemical composition , used in scientific , industrial , or household contexts .

/SUBSTANCE/FOOD
Any substance consumed to provide nutritional support for an organism , including meals , ingredients , and edible products .

/GPE/CITY
A large and significant urban area , typically with its own local government and administrative boundaries .

/GPE/STATE_PROVINCE
A primary administrative division within a country , such as a state in the USA or a province in Canada .

/GPE/COUNTRY
A sovereign nation with defined borders , government , and recognized international status .

/PRODUCT/VEHICLE
A manufactured means of transportation , such as cars , airplanes , ships , or trains .

/PRODUCT/WEAPON
An item designed or used for inflicting harm or damage , including firearms , explosives , and other armaments .

/FACILITIES/BUILDING
A constructed structure with a roof and walls , such as houses , offices , or schools .

/FACILITIES/AIRPORT
A complex for the arrival , departure , and maintenance of aircraft , including runways and terminals .

/FACILITIES/HIGHWAY_STREET
A constructed route for vehicular or pedestrian traffic , such as roads , highways , or streets .

/FACILITIES/ATTRACTION
A place or structure designed to attract visitors for leisure , tourism , or entertainment , such as amusement parks or landmarks .

/FACILITIES/BRIDGE
A structure built to span physical obstacles , such as rivers or roads , for the purpose of providing passage .

/EVENT/HURRICANE
A specific type of natural disaster characterized by a large , powerful tropical cyclone with strong winds and heavy rain .

/EVENT/WAR
A prolonged conflict between countries or groups , typically involving organized armed forces .

/WORK_OF_ART/BOOK
A written or printed literary work , typically bound and consisting of pages , such as novels or textbooks .

/WORK_OF_ART/PLAY
A dramatic composition intended for theatrical performance .

/WORK_OF_ART/SONG
A musical composition with lyrics , intended to be sung .
